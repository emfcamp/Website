What we're looking for

Lightning talks are super short talks on any topic: be it a random tip you think more people should know about to a quick investigation of complex biology.

What it's like

We'll be running lightning talk sessions Friday to Sunday with each session being up to 12 slots each a maxiumum of 5 minutes long.

Selection

Slots are allocated first come, first served. Unless you are told otherwise your lightning talk will be accepted, and you should come to the session to give it. You must submit your slides as a pdf as part of the submission and select which day you'd like to talk.
