Call for Participation

The Call for Participation will close for talks & workshops on Thursday 25th April.

You will still be able to submit installations, performances and youth workshops after this date

Electromagnetic Field is a camping festival for those with an inquisitive mind or an interest in making things. It will be held from {{ fancy_dates }} at Eastnor Castle Deer Park in Herefordshire, UK.

We're looking for talks, performances, and workshops for all ages. We're especially keen to hear from people with different backgrounds than you'd expect to find at a technology conference.

At previous events we've heard about everything from genetic modification to electronics, blacksmithing to high-energy physics, reverse engineering to lock picking, computer security to crocheting, and quadcopters to brewing. If you're enthusiastic about it, we are too! You don't need to consider yourself a professional or an expert to present.

If you submit a talk or workshop proposal, it will be reviewed anonymously by a diverse selection panel.

Money

EMF is a non-profit community event, entirely run by unpaid volunteers. All of the ticket cost goes directly towards turning a field into an amazing place to spend a weekend, and all those who attend contribute to making the event a success.

As with all contributors to EMF, speakers do not receive any payment, although we have some budget to subsidise travel within the UK for those who could not otherwise afford it. We also have some funds available for up-front costs for workshops, for example, to develop kits for attendees.

Tickets

You will be guaranteed the opportunity to buy tickets if your talk, workshop, youth workshop, or performance proposal is accepted. There are a limited number of tickets available for exceptional installations. The guarantee is for two adult tickets: one for you at a 33% discount, and one for a friend or partner at full price. You are also guaranteed as many child tickets as you need.

If you only wish to attend for the day of your talk/workshop/performance, we can offer you a free day ticket, but we encourage you to stick around for the whole event!
