As well as our sponsored crèche, the EMF team also organise a range of workshops for families in a separate workshop tent.

We are looking for activities that will appeal to our younger audience and encourage them to play, experiment and create.

This should be run in a drop-in fashion (i.e. assume that people may join at any time).

In the past, workshops have included programming Lego robots, making UV torches, bridge building competitions, pom pom making, storytelling, and geocaching.

We’d also love to hear from you if you have an idea for an installation that would be suitable for our younger attendees - giant DIY marble runs, interactive inflatable tentacles, giant colouring books, anything goes! If you have an idea for an installation please email family@emfcamp.org.

Practical considerations

  • All children will be accompanied by an adult during workshops.
  • The tent can hold a maximum of 30 people per workshop, including accompanying adults.
  • The family workshop should be run in a drop-in fashion (don't expect people to join at the start or stay until the end).
  • Family workshops are generally one to two hours long, but can vary.
  • Attendance is operated on a first-come-first-served basis.
  • The tent comes equipped with tables, chairs, screen, and PA system.
  • The Family Team are available to provide advice & assistance both before the festival and at any time during the event.
