Speaker tickets to Electromagnetic Field

We're delighted that you're contributing to EMF this year! This page allows you to get the tickets you're entitled to.

If you need more tickets than this, can't afford the tickets you need, or have any other questions about participating at EMF, get in touch and we'll find a solution!

Your ticket gets you:

  • A secure place to camp for three days
  • Very fast internet access, both wired and wireless
  • Mains power
  • Access to running water and hot showers
  • A whole pile of awesome talks and workshops
  • Access to a well-stocked bar, and multiple food vendors
  • One fully programmable camp badge, equipped with wireless communications and screen
  • A campsite filled with weird and wonderful things to see.
  • Free professional childcare & youth activities
  • A weekend unlike any other in the UK

Accessibility: we're committed to making EMF as accessible as we can. If you have any requirements or requests you would like to discuss with us please feel free to email us on {{ config['TICKETS_EMAIL'][1] }}. We offer free tickets for carers; please drop us an email after you've bought your ticket.

Parking: No vehicles other than camper vans are permitted to be parked on site, you will need to buy a car parking ticket if you intend to bring a car. If your vehicle is an integral part of a village or installation please contact us.

By buying a ticket you are agreeing to our Terms and Conditions and the Code of Conduct.
