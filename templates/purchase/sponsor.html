Sponsor

Support EMF with a donation. Small amounts go to our Art & Installations fund, which directly supports art and attendee projects at EMF. Larger donations contribute to our broader sponsorship goals including free childcare, live transcription, and funding for speakers and workshops who would otherwise be unable to attend.

If you would like an acknowledgment on our sponsors page, please send your preferred sponsor name to sponsor@emfcamp.org after paying. Sponsorships will be displayed on the sponsor page. Donations to the Art & Installations fund will be displayed on a special Wall of Fame installation in the field at EMF 2026.

Please note: as these sponsorship tiers only entitle you to an acknowledgment, they are considered a donation which is outside the scope of VAT. No VAT will be charged on your sponsorship.
