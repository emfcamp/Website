Diversity

EMF is well aware of the controversy about the lack of diversity in technology-related events, and we are trying our utmost to improve this.

Although gender diversity is the most-discussed area, we will not be content with an equal gender balance. Diversity of age and social background, among others, are areas we feel are important to address.

We strongly encourage submissions and attendance from all under-represented groups, and we will strive to make sure EMF is a place where every attendee feels safe. We have a documented code of conduct which we will enforce.

Financial Means

EMF is a non-profit community event which is operated on a very slim margin. Our primary source of income is from ticket sales, and we strive to keep ticket prices as low as possible. We hope everyone who wants to speak will find the event compelling enough to buy a ticket and stay for the weekend, as this is an important part of the event.

We have a very limited budget to offer day tickets and pay travel expenses to speakers who would otherwise find it financially prohibitive to attend.
