Read this first!

This page allows you to adjust your role's shift timings by defining a set of templates. As a role admin you're able to see the shift schedule before attendees can by clicking the Schedule button above.

Each template specifies details of the location, time, duration, and volunteer requirements for a given time period. You can have multiple templates for a single day if your volunteer requirements will change over the course of a day, and you can have templates for multiple locations.

The duration field specifies how long a shift should be, changeover specifies the amount of overlap with the preceeding shift to allow volunteer briefing and handover. Unless you have a good reason otherwise please try to keep shifts to 2 hours.

All days are event days with Day 0 being the Wednesday before gates open, and run from 5am to 5am so that late night shifts show up in the volunteer schedule for the day before.

Once you check the finalise shifts box and save your shifts will be considered ready to sign people up for, and they will be made visible to all volunteers once shift signup is opened. You will not be able to safely edit these templates once that happens so double check you're happy first.
