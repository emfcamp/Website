# How to use the till

There are several till terminals behind the bar. They’re all interconnected, which means your transaction follows you between them.

When you turn up to work on the bar, we’ll give you an NFC wristband. This lets you log in to the till every time you use it. Just tap the wristband on the reader, and your transaction will appear on the screen.

When you step away from the till, you can press the Lock button on the keyboard to prevent anyone else from altering your transaction by mistake if they forget to log in themselves. You should get in the habit of tapping the wristband every time you step up to the till and pressing Lock every time you step away.

The till display will look something like this:

<img src="/static/images/bar/till-buttons.png">

Your transaction on the till can be either open or closed. When it’s open, it’s incomplete, and you need to finish it before continuing, usually by telling the till how it was paid. When it’s closed, it’s finished, and you’re ready to start a new transaction.

The transaction state is shown after your name in the red status line at the top of the screen. If there’s nothing there, you don’t have a current transaction and can start a new one.

An easy way to check whether the current transaction is closed is to press the Clear button. If it’s closed, it will disappear; if it’s open, nothing will happen. It can be a good habit to press Clear and check that the transaction disappears from the screen before starting a new one.
