# Ice
The ice buckets on the back bar will contain ice. If empty, ask the bar manager to refill them from the chest freezer.

When you need ice, use the metal scoop or tongs provided to scoop it out of the bucket.

!!! warning "Never use a cup as a scoop"
	Scooping with the cup could chip the rim, leaving small, sharp pieces of glass in a bucket of clear ice and water.
	
	Additionally, this can result in the ice and cup being contaminated.
