# Cups
We provide reusable polypropylene cups to avoid the waste of single-use plastic cups. 

Customers who bring their cups or reuse one of our cups will be eligible for a small discount on the price of their drink.

!!! warning "Hygenic cup reuse"
	We must not allow the spout of the taps or pumps to touch the drink or cup.

	For spirits, use a thimble to avoid contaminating the optics through cup reuse.

## Bring Your Own Cup
Customers can bring their cups for us to pour into. For beer, these must display a Crown, UKCA or CE mark etched or printed on the cup. Unless the cup reads “(half) pint to line” it is (half) pint to brim.

If you need clarification, please ask a bar manager.

## Cup Reuse
Customers can reuse a cup, but we will not clean it for them, we have partnered with a company called Instarinse who will loan us their high speed cup washing machines for the event which customers can use themselves.

## Handling Cups
When you pick up one of these cups to pour into, please hold it by the bottom. Your fingers should be nowhere near the rim of the cup.
