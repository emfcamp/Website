# Bar Tasks
[TOC]

Between busy periods or as needed, you can prepare for the next rush by performing essential maintenance tasks on the bar, including:

## Restocking the Fridge
Restocking is often easier in pairs. Write down what product you need and collect it from the fridge. 

!!! danger "Don't injury your back"
	Don’t be a hero, save your back and an injury.

	Use a trolley or avoid carrying more than 1 large or 2 small boxes at a time.

	Always lift with your legs, and keep your back straight.

## Collecting Empties
All empty EMF hire cups must be returned at the end of the event. These should be stacked out the back in the provided eurocrate containers.

!!! info "Help us save money"
	Every lost, stolen or damaged hire cup costs EMF about 50p in fees, so please do everything you can to return them.

## Wiping Down the Bar
Wiping down the bar is pretty self-explanatory:

1. Clear the bar of clutter.

2. Use the spray cleaner at the sink and wipe it down with a cloth.
Avoid spraying near open cups and people serving pints, which could get into the drink.

!!! warning "Wash your hands after cleaning the bar"
	Wash your hands before serving customers if you have been involved in wiping down the bar.

## Emptying Drip Trays
As drip trays become full under the taps and pumps, they must be emptied and rinsed. Each drip tray is equivalent to 1 pint of wasted product, which needs to be recorded for stock control.

The procedure for emptying a drip tray is:

1. Carefully bring the tray to the sink and empty it.

2. Rinse the tray and grille with clean, warm water only.

3. Reinstall the tray.

4. Log in to a till terminal and press "Record Waste".
<img src="/static/images/bar/till-waste-button.png" width="100px">

5. Press the button of the waste to record, for example, "Pint Tap 1":
<img src="/static/images/bar/till-record-waste.png" class="image-left">

6. Set Waste reason to "Drip Tray" by pressing Enter on that field and selecting from the list:
<img src="/static/images/bar/till-waste-options.png" class="image-left">

7. Enter the amount wasted as "1" pints:
<img src="/static/images/bar/till-enter-waste.png" class="image">

8. Press "Enter" to confirm.
<img src="/static/images/bar/till-waste-recorded.png" class="image">

9. Repeat for each full drip tray. Avoid preemptively emptying drip trays.

!!! warning "Excessive drip tray emptying costs us money"
	Let a bar manager know if you have to empty a particular tray a lot as it could indicate an issue with the product.
