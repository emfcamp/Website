# Cider

Most of our ciders will be served from bag-in-box containers. We’ll aim to have a dry, medium and sweet cider while stock lasts.

## Procedure
The procedure for serving cider is:

1. Hold the cup under the spout.

2. Lift the coloured plastic piece upwards to serve.

3. Release when full.

4. Put the full cup on the counter.

As the box nears empty, you should ask for help tilting it to remove the last product more quickly.
