# Bar Training Guide
Thank you for volunteering to help at the bar at EMF. 

This is the training guide for the bars and is in addition to the [mandatory legal training that all bar volunteers must undertake before arriving at the bar](/volunteer/bar-training).

If you’ve ever worked on a bar before many things in this guide will already be familiar. If you haven’t, this guide should let you know what to expect.

In either case, when you turn up at the bar, we’ll review the important points and check that you understand them before serving drinks.
