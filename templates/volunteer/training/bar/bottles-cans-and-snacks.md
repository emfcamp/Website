# Bottles, cans and snacks
There will be bottles of Club Mate, cans, and bottles of other hard and soft drinks. Some of these have crown corks; there are bottle openers behind the bar. We may also have bar snacks.

Please offer ice when serving bottles, and remember to use the scoop.

When you sell a bottle, it’s essential that you put the correct variety in the till. The till keeps count of how many bottles of each type we have in the fridge so it can print restocking lists; if you put the wrong variety in, then these lists will be wrong. (For example, you sell a regular Club Mate but put it in as Club Mate Granat. The restocking list will call for 1 too many bottles of Granat and 1 too few bottles of Regular.)
