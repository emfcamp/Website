# Things to know
Stuff you only find out after volunteering too many times…

## Club Mate
Club Mate is a caffeinated herbal extract from Germany. It is sold at EMF in two varieties: original (blue cap) and Granat (red cap), which is pomegranate-flavoured.

People come to the bar asking for this with various regional pronunciations.

## Jigger
This is another word for Thimble, which refers to the measures used to pour wines and spirits on the back bar.

## Tschunk
Pronounced [sh-un-k], tschunk is a cocktail popular with our German hacker crowd, particularly on the last night of EMF.
To prepare Tschunk:

1. Add a teaspoon of brown sugar.
2. Square a piece of lime into the drink and leave the piece in the cup.
3. Squish with the muddler into the cup.
4. Pour a single shot of Havana Club 7 (dark rum) into the cup.
5. Place a small amount of ice in a half-pint cup.
6. Fill with Club Mate
7. Present the cup and remaining bottle to the customer.

!!! info
	A single Club Mate can make up to 4x Tschunks. Check with the customer if they want to save money by splitting the bottle of Club Mate between cocktails, in which case they only charge for the single rum as the ice, sugar, and lime are free.
