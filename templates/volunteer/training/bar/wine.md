# Wine

Wine is served in measures of 125ml, 175ml or 250ml, or as whole bottles. Our cups aren’t marked for these quantities, so all the wine we serve by the cup has to be measured using the 125ml and 175ml thimble measures kept on the back bar.

# Procedure
The procedure for serving wine is:

1. Select the correct thimble size to achieve the desired measure.
250ml is achieved with 2 x 125ml servings.

2. Fill the thimble to the brim.

3. Confidently pour the thimble into the cup.

4. Repeat for multiple measures.

5. Put the cap back on the wine.

6. Wash the thimble(s) in the sink.

7. Leave to dry upside down on the metal tray.
