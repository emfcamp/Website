# Spirits
Most spirit bottles will be on optics measuring exact 25ml servings. Any other spirits can be served the same way as wine but using the 25ml thimble.

# Procedure
The procedure for serving spirits from an optic is:

1. Ensure the optic is full.

2. Hold the cup underneath and push it up.

2. Wait for the optic to completely empty and stop dripping.

3. Release the optic.

5. Repeat for a double, ensuring you wait for the optic to refill entirely.

!!! warning "Stock replenishment"
	When you finish a bottle of spirits, please do not start the next one, even if you can see it.
	
	Tell a bar manager, and they will tell the till that we’ve started a new bottle. This helps us keep track of how much we have left.

!!! warning "Handling an optic with a bottle attached"
	If you ever need to take an optic off the bracket, make sure that you hold the optic in one hand and the bottle in the other.

	Don’t ever hold a bottle upside down without keeping hold of the optic: if you do, the optic will likely fall out, and you’ll end up with expensive wet feet.
