About

The Night Market is a low-key bazaar where you can admire and buy wares of makers from a wide range of backgrounds!

Night Market stalls are typically open from 6-11pm, Friday to Sunday. Some stalls will stay open until 1am to catch the late crowds.

The Night Market is located away from the dance floor, so you can still get your accessories into the evening. However, if you prefer a quieter time, we recommend coming around 6-7pm.

Hexpansion Day Market

We’ll also be hosting a day market exclusively for buying, trading, and selling badge Hexpansions from 2-4pm on Saturday, and again from 10am-midday on Sunday. This day market will take over the dance floor of NullSector, to allow enough space for everyone.

Bring your own Hexpansion to trade or sell, or just your pocket money to seek out awesome plug-ins. And if you're selling, please bring whatever you need to help you hold and display your stock so you can comfortably perform any transactions (e.g. a wearable display so you have your hands free).

Payment

Night Market vendors will all accept card payment, no need for cash or Polybius Credits.
