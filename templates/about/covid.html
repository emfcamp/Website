COVID-19

Revision 1, published 2022-01-12

This page is under constant review and may change at short notice.

We are following the COVID-19 situation closely, and much has changed since we made the decision to cancel EMF 2020.

Much more is likely to change (hopefully for the better) in the run-up to the event, but whatever happens we will be taking steps to ensure the safety of EMF attendees and volunteers.

Given the rapidly changing situation we cannot yet specify what measures will be implemented on site - the COVID precautions we take at EMF will be governed by the prevalence closer to the time, as well as any government restrictions in force.

We will clearly announce the measures in place before the event, and any recommendations we have for attendees to ensure both their safety and the safety of others.

In the meantime we encourage you to get vaccinated & boosted as soon as possible, and test yourself regularly.
