Contact

If you need to get in touch with us, first see if your query is best directed to one of the teams below:

  • Content, for talks, workshops, and installations
  • Volunteering
  • Sponsorship
  • Food/drink vendors
  • Villages

For general questions or if you're not sure which team is most appropriate, please email contact@emfcamp.org.

We may take a while to respond, especially if you're contacting us when we're not actively planning an event — we're all volunteers!
